Three fit routines are provided with specialised input/output configurations:

  • Fiting of separate sepctra (1D) ccd_single
  • Fitting of time-regularized spectra (2D) ccd_time

Each fit routine can use one of the following kernels (i.e., models) to describe the SIP spectra:

  • Resistivity Cole-Cole decomposition
  • Conductivity Cole-Cole decomposition
  • ccd_single
  • ccd_time
  • ddps.py
