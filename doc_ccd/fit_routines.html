Three fit routines are provided with specialised input/output configurations:

  • Fiting of separate sepctra (1D) dd_single.py
  • Fitting of time-regularized spectra (2D) dd_time.py

Each fit routine can use one of the following kernels (i.e., models) to describe the SIP spectra:

  • Resistivity Debye decomposition
  • Conductivity Debye decomposition
